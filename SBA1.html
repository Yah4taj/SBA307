LEARNING AND GROWING


  • Course 1 Preview: Discovering Your Purpose

  • Course 2 Preview: You Are What You Think: Metacognition

  • Course 3 Preview: Watch Your Words:The Power of Speech

  • Course 4 Preview: An Authentic Image: How Do You Show Up in the World?


Course 1 Description: The path to discovering your calling should be just as exciting as living it out. Sometimes we focus so much on arriving that we forget to simply enjoy the journey. This course will provide you with the practical steps to appreciating the process.


Course 2 Description: It's so important to be aware of what you're thinking. The way we think about a situation, place, person or thing impact out interactions. The key to shifting what you think is to target how you think. This course will provide you with the necessary tools to monitor and assess your thoughts, feelings and reasoning.


Course 3 Description: What you say is just as important as how you say it. You reap what you sow is not just pertainig to actions but words as well. The words you speak have the power to shape your experience whether positive or negative. This course examines the importance of being intentional about the words we release and where we place our agreement.


Course 4 Description: Image is more than how you outwardly appear. How you show up internally impacts how you show up in the world. Although style has a part to play in a person's image, each person brings whi they are to what they wear. This course examines the introspective nature of image and personal style.
